## 1. Top 5 Sneaker Nam Xu Huong 2024

Category: Xu huong  
Tags: sneaker, nam, streetwear

Nam 2024, xu huong sneaker nam tap trung vao tinh ung dung, kha nang phoi do va cam giac mang thoai mai. Thay vi chi chon nhung doi giay qua noi bat, nhieu nguoi uu tien cac mau co the dung trong nhieu hoan canh: di hoc, di lam, di choi va gap ban be. Khi chon sneaker nam, ban nen can nhac ba yeu to: nhu cau su dung, mau sac trong tu do va do vua chan. Mot doi giay dep nhung kho phoi se it duoc su dung. Nguoc lai, mot doi sneaker vua chan, mau de mac va chat lieu de cham soc se mang lai gia tri lau dai hon.

## 2. Cach Ve Sinh Giay Trang Tai Nha Dung Cach

Category: Huong dan  
Tags: ve sinh, giay trang, sneaker care

Giay trang de phoi do nhung cung rat de bam bui, vi vay viec ve sinh dung cach giup giay ben va giu mau tot hon. Truoc khi lam sach, ban nen thao day giay va lot giay ra rieng. Dung ban chai mem phu bui tren be mat, tranh cha qua manh khi giay con kho vi co the lam xuoc chat lieu da hoac vai.

Voi than giay da tong hop, co the dung khan am ket hop dung dich ve sinh giay chuyen dung. Lau theo vong tron nho, tap trung vao vung bam banh va mui giay. Voi de giay, dung ban chai long cung hon de lam sach cac khe. Neu day giay bi ban, co the ngam rieng trong nuoc am voi mot it xa phong nhe, sau do xa sach va phoi noi thoang.

Khong nen ngam ca doi giay trong nuoc, khong dung chat tay manh va khong phoi truc tiep duoi nang gat. Nhiet do cao co the lam vang de, cung keo dan va bien dang form giay. Sau khi ve sinh, nhet giay bang giay trang hoac khan kho de giu form, roi phoi noi mat. Neu muon bao quan lau dai, ban nen ve sinh nhe sau moi vai lan mang thay vi doi den khi giay qua ban.

## 3. Nike vs Adidas: Thuong Hieu Nao Tot Hon?

Category: Review  
Tags: nike, adidas, review

Nike va Adidas deu la hai thuong hieu giay the thao lon, nhung moi hang co diem manh rieng. Nike thuong noi bat o thiet ke tre trung, nhan dien manh va nhieu dong sneaker mang tinh bieu tuong nhu Air Force 1, Air Max va Jordan. Cac mau Nike thuong de tao diem nhan cho outfit, phu hop voi nguoi thich streetwear va phong cach nang dong.

Adidas lai co loi the o nhung mau giay toi gian va cong nghe dem nhu Boost. Ultra Boost phu hop voi nguoi can cam giac em khi di bo nhieu, trong khi Stan Smith la mau lifestyle gon gang, de dung trong moi truong hoc tap hoac cong so. Adidas thuong duoc chon boi nguoi thich thiet ke sach, nhe va it qua lo.

Khong co cau tra loi tuyet doi cho viec thuong hieu nao tot hon. Neu ban uu tien thiet ke noi bat, nhan dien manh va kha nang phoi streetwear, Nike la lua chon phu hop. Neu ban muon cam giac em, thiet ke toi gian va tinh ung dung cao, Adidas dang de can nhac. Dieu quan trong nhat la chon dung mau giay cho nhu cau that, dung size va ngan sach.

## 4. Giay Running vs Giay Lifestyle: Khac Nhau The Nao?

Category: Kien thuc  
Tags: running, lifestyle, chon giay

Giay running va giay lifestyle co the nhin giong nhau o mot so mau, nhung muc dich thiet ke khac ro. Giay running uu tien trong luong nhe, dem em, do thoang khi va kha nang ho tro chuyen dong tien ve phia truoc. Nhung mau nhu Adidas Ultra Boost 22 hay Nike Air Max 270 phu hop voi nguoi di bo nhieu, chay nhe hoac can cam giac em trong thoi gian dai.

Giay lifestyle tap trung vao tinh thoi trang va kha nang phoi do. New Balance 574, Adidas Stan Smith hay Nike Air Force 1 co form dep, de mac voi nhieu outfit va phu hop su dung hang ngay. Tuy nhien, chung khong phai luc nao cung toi uu cho chay bo nghiem tuc vi trong luong, do on dinh va cau truc de khac voi giay running.

Neu ban mua giay de tap luyen, hay uu tien running shoes dung muc dich. Neu ban can giay di hoc, di lam, di choi va chup anh outfit, lifestyle shoes se phu hop hon. Mot tu giay can bang co the co mot doi running em chan va mot doi lifestyle de phoi do.
